About Me

I'm a data-driven product analyst and quantitative researcher based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with a unique blend of statistical rigor and engineering execution. My journey started with a deep love for Statistics at the University of Dhaka, where I earned both my BSc and MSc, before diving into Data Science through an MS in Computer Science & Engineering.

At FundedNext, one of the fastest-growing prop trading firms globally, I've progressed from BI Analyst to Senior Analyst, owning the entire product analytics lifecycle — from acquisition funnels to funded trader performance. I architect data pipelines, design A/B tests, build executive dashboards, and deploy ML models that directly impact business outcomes.

Beyond my day job, I work as a Quant Researcher at RemoteDevs, where I optimize systematic futures trading strategies using ML-driven signal models. I also founded Alpha Analyst, a data analytics consultancy serving clients with quantitative research, predictive modelling, and BI solutions.

I believe in the power of data to transform decisions — whether it's identifying high-risk trading accounts, automating KYC verification with AI, or building recommendation systems that guide career paths.
